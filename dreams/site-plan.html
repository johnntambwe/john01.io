Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform people about not all, but some few hotels where people can dream their dreams in my town. The site will have just a name, a photo and the place where you can find the hotel not the adress. All informations here will not be under copyright ©. The purpose here is not to advertise or making any kind of marketing.

Everyone in my home town, foreigners and tourists who want to visit my country.
